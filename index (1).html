🔹 Interesting Facts About Makhana

  • Makhana is actually a seed, not a nut, and comes from the Euryale Ferox plant.
  • It is harvested from water bodies in a unique and labor-intensive process.
  • India is the largest producer and consumer of makhana in the world.
  • Makhana has been used in Ayurveda for over 2000 years for its medicinal properties.
  • It contains kaempferol, a natural compound known for anti-aging and anti-inflammatory benefits.
  • Makhana is often used in religious rituals and fasting meals due to its purity and health benefits.
